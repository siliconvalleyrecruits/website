About Us

At Silicon Valley Recruits, our core mission is “connecting top talent with the right opportunities.” We focus on leveraging our extensive professional network instead of relying solely on standard search platforms. Over the years, we have cultivated strong connections with professionals across various industries and locations.

Our emphasis is on providing high-quality recruitment rather than high-volume placement, assisting businesses seeking specialized, dedicated talent. Our systematic approach ensures each placement aligns with both the client’s requirements and the candidate’s career aspirations.

Our Services

Permanent Staffing

Silicon Valley Recruits offers permanent staffing solutions with fully screened and qualified candidates across any industry. Our dedicated team of recruiters strives to help your company meet its business objectives. Our process guarantees we source the best candidates at any level with the quickest turnaround time.

Contract Staffing

Our employee leasing service transfers your employees onto the payroll of Silicon Valley Recruits, then leases them back to you on a temporary basis. This makes us the legal employer, taking over all HR administrative and management responsibilities.

By outsourcing HR administration to us, you can focus on your core business while saving money, time, and resources. We can provide the right people for any duration or role.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing

We assume complete ownership of your staffing requirements, ensuring resources are onboarded according to agreed timelines. With a dedicated team focusing on your assignment, we deliver timely, cost-effective solutions while maintaining a high-quality candidate pool.

Our scope includes everything from analyzing business needs to onboarding, effectively planning resources, and adjusting recruiter strength to match varying business demands.
